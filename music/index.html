Music

Background

Music has formed a huge and fundamental part of my life. I’ve enjoyed playing the piano from a young age, and got into composition during middle-school where my music teacher introduced me to the creative wonderland that is Logic Pro.

I spent a while in high-school composing piano pieces, then switching to predominantly electronic music production during university. Since graduating, I’ve been working on predominantly producing quantity over ‘quality’ due to my belief — and now experience — that constantly creating provides a great deal more opportunity to learn and develop than agonising over the finer details of one piece.
